Privacy Policy — DocDoor

Effective Date: May 21, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 21, 2026  ·  Operated by Divya Tech

📋 Introduction

Welcome to DocDoor, operated by Divya Tech. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using DocDoor, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

🗂️ 1. Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on the role you use the app as.

All Users

  • Full name, mobile number (primary login ID), and email address
  • Profile picture — optional, only if you choose to upload one
  • State and district of residence; account status flags
  • Device and login information — device type, OS version, login timestamps
  • App usage data — screens visited, features used (for support and debugging)
  • Push notification device token — for appointment and system alerts
  • Aadhaar verification status — we only store a yes/no flag. Your Aadhaar number and images are processed by the UIDAI-verified provider and discarded by us.
We do not collect medical records, diagnoses, prescriptions, or any clinical health data.

Patient

  • Appointment history (dates, times, assigned doctor or hospital, status, your notes)
  • Reviews and ratings you leave for doctors or hospitals
  • Bookmarks (doctors / hospitals you save for later)
  • Identity verification state (mobile OTP, email verification)
  • No-show / cancellation history (used for the freeze-on-repeat-no-show policy)

Doctor

  • Medical council licence / registration number; specialisation; qualifications; years of experience; bio; languages spoken
  • Practice location: state, district, subdivision, clinic or hospital name, full address, consultation fee, working days and hours
  • Aadhaar QR code image (name and date of birth confirmation; stored for audit); medical council registration certificate; matching flags
  • Availability slots, daily slots, follow-up slots, urgent-booking flags
  • Appointments booked with you (patient name, phone, date/time, status, notes, review)
  • Aggregated statistics (average rating, total reviews, total appointments, verification badge)

Hospital Admin

  • Hospital profile: name, description, phone, email, logo, full address, state, district, pincode, latitude/longitude, verification status
  • Hospital–doctor associations: invitations sent, per-doctor consultation fee overrides
  • Hospital availability: day-wise and date-range availability, slot duration, capacity, booking counts
  • Patient appointments, prescriptions, payments (amount, status, gateway, transaction ID, refund details), walk-in reservations
  • Aggregated hospital statistics and admin/impersonation logs

⚙️ 2. Why We Collect It

  • Account creation, authentication, and role-based access control
  • Identity validation (mobile OTP, email verification, Aadhaar-based verification for doctors)
  • Helping patients find and book the right doctor or hospital
  • Appointment booking, management, reminders (SMS, email, push), and follow-ups
  • Processing payments, refunds, and consultation fees
  • Detecting fraud, abuse, and repeat no-shows
  • Customer and technical support
  • Internal analytics in aggregate form to improve the service
Your data is never used for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any commercial purpose unrelated to this service.

🔒 3. How We Protect It

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Data at rest is protected with AES-256 encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a unique salt — never stored in plain text
  • Access to authentication records is restricted to role-authorised internal services only
  • Identity verification images are stored encrypted and accessible only to the verification workflow and authorised reviewers
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are performed

🤝 4. Who We Share It With

All Users

  • SMS / email / push notification providers — receive your phone number, email, or device token solely to deliver alerts on our behalf
  • Encrypted cloud infrastructure — data is stored in secure, encrypted cloud environments

Patient

  • Your assigned doctor or hospital — receives your appointment details
  • Other patients (anonymously) — see only anonymised statistics on doctor/hospital profiles

Doctor

  • Patients searching — see your public profile: name, specialisation, qualifications, languages, fee, working hours, average rating
  • Hospital admin you associate with — sees your professional details and availability

Hospital Admin

  • Patients searching — see your public hospital profile: name, description, logo, address, verification status, associated doctors
  • Doctors you invite — see your invitation notes and hospital name
We never sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose your personal data to any third party for their own purposes.

✅ 5. Your Rights

  • View — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correct — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Export — receive your data in a portable format
  • Freeze — your account may be temporarily frozen if repeated no-shows are detected. Strike history is tied to your phone number and persists across account deletion.
  • Delete — request deletion of your account. Re-registration with the same credentials is restricted for 30 days. Personally identifying details are removed.
For doctors and hospital admins, deleting your account does not automatically remove appointment, prescription, payment, or review records you created — those remain for legal and audit purposes, tied to "Deleted User".

To exercise any of these rights, go to Settings → Privacy in the app, or contact us at support.docdoor@gmail.com.

🗓️ 6. Retention

  • Future appointments are cancelled immediately upon account deletion; the account is deactivated
  • Personally identifying information is permanently erased within 30 days of deletion
  • Appointment history visible to the other party is retained as "Deleted User" after 30 days
  • Review ratings are retained anonymously; review comments are removed after 30 days
  • Prescriptions, payment records, and audit logs are retained for up to 7 years as required by financial and medical regulations, with personal identifiers removed after 30 days
  • System and access logs are purged after 12 months

🔗 Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — for push notifications
  • SMS / email notification providers — for appointment reminders
  • UIDAI-verified Aadhaar provider — for identity verification (doctors only); Aadhaar numbers and images are processed by the provider and discarded by us
  • Encrypted cloud infrastructure — for secure data storage
  • Google Play Services — for app distribution and device authentication

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services. DocDoor is not responsible for the data practices of third-party providers.

👶 Children's Privacy

DocDoor is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support.docdoor@gmail.com.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Last Updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of DocDoor after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
